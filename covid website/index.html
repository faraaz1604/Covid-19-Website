take steps to protect yourself

Wear A Face Mask

Masks should be used as part of a comprehensive strategy of measures to suppress transmission and save lives; the use of a mask alone is not sufficient to provide an adequate level of protection against COVID-19.

Learn more

Wash your Hands

According to the CDC, washing your hands frequently is particularly important to help prevent the spread of the new coronavirus known as SARS-CoV-2, which causes the disease known as COVID-19.

Learn more

Avoid Close Contact

Stay away from people who are sick. Sick people can spread viruses that cause the common cold through close contact with others.
